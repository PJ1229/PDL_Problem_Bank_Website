You can take a look at some of our Teen's solutions on this page. To submit a problem, click the "home" button on the top. Letter Box:

Problem:
"We could use a box with sections for the sandwich board
letters. Right now each letter is rubber-banded and the
bands keep drying out and breaking. A sectioned box like
the seed box would be a nice way to keep the letters and
numbers sorted. This would make it WAY easier to grab the
letters as you aremaking a message and easier to sort
them back once you are taking the message off the board."

Solution:
Our solution was to create a wooden box to hold the letters
The box has individual containers for each type of letter,
and they are easy to access. This was created using the
Glowforge laser cutting machine at the lab. the Glowforge
is free to use here at the Plymouth District Library.
